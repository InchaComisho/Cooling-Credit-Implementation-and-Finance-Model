## Interpretation

The business models explain **who can participate, what is restored, how value is created, and what revenue sources may exist**.

The simulations provide conceptual testbeds for comparing:

- no-action burden,
- conventional development,
- cooling-focused implementation,
- natural feedback recovery,
- local economic co-benefits,
- MRV-dependent Cooling Credit value.

These models are not forecasts. They are transparent conceptual simulations intended to be replaced with local measured data, MRV, and independent review before real-world decision-making.
